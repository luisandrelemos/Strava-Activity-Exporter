Privacy Policy

Data Access
This app accesses your Strava activities through the official Strava API with your authorization.

Data Storage
Your data is only stored temporarily in your browser session. No data is stored on any server. When you logout or close the browser, all data is cleared.

Data Usage
Your activity data is used solely to display and export your activities. We do not share, sell, or analyze your data.

Third Parties
This app does not share your data with any third parties.

Revoke Access
You can revoke this app's access to your Strava account at any time in your Strava Settings.
